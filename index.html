    Overview

    Welcome to Calc-X, a versatile scientific calculator designed to handle everything from basic arithmetic to advanced trigonometric and logarithmic functions. Whether you're a student, engineer, or math enthusiast, Calc-X has you covered.

    Usage Guidelines

    • Input: Click the buttons or type your expression directly from keyboard.
    • Clear: Use "AC" to reset the calculator.
    • Backspace: "⌫" removes the last character or function (e.g., "sin(").
    • Mode: Toggle between "DEG" (degrees) and "RAD" (radians) for trigonometric functions.
    • Evaluate: Press "=" to compute the result.
    • History: Access past calculations via the history button.
